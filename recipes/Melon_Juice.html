MELON JUICE

Description:

Melon Juice is a refreshing Filipino beverage made from shredded cantaloupe mixed with sweetened water and a hint of vanilla. Perfect for warm days, this drink is light, hydrating, and easy to prepare. With its natural sweetness and refreshing taste, it’s a favorite among kids and adults alike, especially when served ice-cold.

  • Prep:15 minutes
  • Cook:1 minute
  • Total:16 minutes
  • Servings:6

Ingredients:

  • 1 small cantaloupe
  • ¾ to 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 10 to 12 cups water
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Slice the cantaloupe in half and remove the seeds.
  2. Scrape the cantaloupe into shreds using a scraper or melon baller.
  3. In a large pitcher, mix water, sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Add the shredded cantaloupe and mix well.
  5. Serve with ice cubes. Enjoy!
