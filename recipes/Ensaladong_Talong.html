EGGPLANT SALAD (Ensaladang Talong)

Description:

Eggplant Salad, or Ensaladang Talong, is a traditional Filipino side dish that pairs beautifully with grilled and fried dishes. It features smoky grilled eggplant mashed to perfection, combined with fresh tomatoes, onions, and tangy vinegar for a burst of flavor. Often topped with shrimp paste, this salad is a refreshing and savory complement to hearty meals.

  • Prep:5 minutes
  • Cook:10 minutes
  • Total: minutes
  • Servings: 3

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces Chinese eggplant
  • 2 pieces tomatoes, cubed
  • 1 piece onion, cubed
  • ¾ cup white vinegar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste (bagoong)

Steps:

  1. Grill eggplant or cook directly on the stovetop.
  2. Peel the skin, cut off the stem, and mash the eggplant lightly with a fork.
  3. Combine mashed eggplant, tomatoes, onion, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Toss to mix.
  4. Top with shrimp paste. Serve and enjoy!

Notes:

Customizable Flavor: You can enhance the flavor by adding bagoong (fermented shrimp paste), which pairs wonderfully with grilled or fried dishes. This salad offers a refreshing complement to heavier main courses.
